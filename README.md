This reposetory houses all the files needed during the development of my replica Bletchely Bombe. The initial plan was to build a fully mechanical 6 bank Bombe (similar to the original, just smaller), but after running simulations 6 banks is not enough to determine a solution and the Bombe stops at every letter. So this version siphons off the mechanical logic to a raspberry pi (that runs the logic in the same way the mechanical Bombe would) allowing it to us all 3 rows of 12 banks that the original Bombe used. The dials on the front of this device are the indicator dials - showing the Enigma's rotor settings for a possible solution.
